# Amazon-Product-Sales-Analysis

**Project Overview**

This project focuses on analyzing Amazon product sales data using an interactive dashboard.
The goal of this dashboard is to understand sales performance, product trends, and customer engagement to support better business decisions.

**Key Features**

- Displays Year-to-Date (YTD) and Quarter-to-Date (QTD) sales performance

- Shows total products sold and customer reviews

- Analyzes monthly and weekly sales trends

- Provides category-wise sales analysis to identify top-performing categories

- Highlights Top 5 products by sales and Top 5 products by reviews

- Includes interactive filters for product category and quarter

**Insights Gained**

- Men Shoes and Camera categories contribute the highest sales

- Sales increase towards the end of the year, showing seasonal demand

- Products with high reviews also show strong customer interest
